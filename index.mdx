# Welcome to Coraltalk

Coraltalk is an AI-powered platform for **conversational learning** and **oral assessment**. Teachers upload course materials, configure a 24/7 teaching assistant, and assign voice-first activities—oral tests, roleplays, and video tickets—so you can see what students truly understand, not just what they typed.

## Core features

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Oral Exams" icon="microphone" href="/assessments/oral-assignments">

  </Card>

  <Card title="Roleplay scenarios" icon="masks-theater">

  <Card title="Vid Tickets" icon="video">
