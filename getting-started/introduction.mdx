# Welcome to Coraltalk

Coraltalk is an AI-powered platform for **conversational learning** and **oral assessment**. Teachers upload course materials, configure a 24/7 teaching assistant, and assign voice-first activities—oral tests, roleplays, and video tickets—so you can see what students truly understand, not just what they typed.

## What you can do in a class

Each **class** (teaching assistant / TA) is a workspace with its own content, students, and tools:

<Accordion title="24/7 Teaching Assistant">
  Students ask questions grounded in your syllabus and materials. You review conversations and summaries to understand what they're struggling with.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Oral Assignments">
  One-to-one voice conversations with Coral, graded against your rubric. Perfect for checking conceptual understanding beyond written essays.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Roleplay Scenarios">
  Scenario-based speaking practice (e.g., investor vs. founder, language practice). Students engage in realistic conversations with AI.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Vid Tickets">
  Async video prompts with peer replies and AI transcripts (similar to Flipgrid, with richer follow-up).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Live Oral Exams">
  Scheduled high-stakes oral assessments with proctoring-style flows where enabled.
</Accordion>

## How to get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [coraltalk.com](https://coraltalk.com). Choose whether you're an educator or student.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Set up your first class">
    Upload your syllabus and configure your AI teaching assistant (Coral).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Invite students">
    Share your class link or class code with students.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create assignments">
    Build oral tests, roleplays, or video tickets for your students.
  </Step>
</Steps>

- **Zoom** — Record lectures and track attendance automatically
