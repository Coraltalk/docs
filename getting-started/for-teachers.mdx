This guide walks through setup and daily use for instructors using Coraltalk on their own or within a school organization.

## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [coraltalk.com](https://coraltalk.com) and choose **Sign up**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select educator role">
    Choose **I'm an educator** (on mobile you may be taken to a dedicated educator signup page)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose authentication">
    Sign up with **email** (verification code) or **Google**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create your first class">
    After signup, you'll be guided to **upload your syllabus** and create your first **class** (teaching assistant)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If you were invited to a school, accept the invitation first — you will land on **home** and can create courses/classes inside the org.
</Note>

## Home and class list

**Home** shows:

- **My Courses** (organization teachers) — select a course, then see its classes
- **Self-paced learning** (where enabled) — independent practice outside a formal class
- **Create new class** / **Create new course** — empty states when you have nothing yet

Click a class card to open that class. Teachers land on the class **Summary** page with quick links to assessments and TA setup.

## Sidebar navigation (inside a class)

Typical teacher items:

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Home / Summary** | Dashboard overview |
| **Oral Assessments** | Manage oral assignment list |
| **Roleplay** | Create and review roleplay exams |
| **Vid Tickets** | Video ticket activities |
| **Chats** | Review student conversations with the TA |
| **Customize** | TA personality, knowledge, settings |
| **Class List** | Student roster and invitations |
| **Curriculum** | Upload syllabus and course materials |
| **Usage** | Monitor minutes and plan consumption |
| **Integrations** | Canvas LTI and API connection |

<Info>Exact items depend on your plan and organization settings</Info>

## Invite students to your class

Use **Copy student link** in the sidebar to share a join URL. Students can also use a **class code** from **Class List**.

<Tip>
Send the class link via your LMS announcement, email, or share it during the first day of class.
</Tip>

## Create assessments

<Accordion title="Oral assignments">
  Best for explaining understanding, defending written work, or structured oral Q&A. Students have one-to-one conversations with Coral who evaluates against your rubric.
  
  [Learn more about oral assignments](/assessments/oral-assignments)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Roleplay">
  Best for scenarios, negotiation practice, language conversation. Students engage in realistic role-based dialogues.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Vid tickets">
  Best for quick video responses and peer replies. Similar to Flipgrid but with deeper engagement.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Live oral exams">
  Scheduled exam sessions when your institution uses them. High-stakes assessments with optional proctoring features.
</Accordion>

## Review student work

Open an assignment to see **submissions**, AI **evaluation**, grades, and transcripts. Use filters for unwatched or by grade band. 

<Info>
Oral assignment settings let you hide evaluation or grades from students while keeping feedback visible.
</Info>

## Independent vs. school teacher

| Feature | Independent | School organization |
|---------|-------------|---------------------|
| First class | Syllabus flow from signup | Create **course**, then **class** |
| Billing | Your subscription per class | Often org-level billing |
| Team | N/A | **Team** in org sidebar |
| Canvas | Optional personal Integrations | LTI + org Integrations |

## Tips for success

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Upload quality materials">
    Upload quality syllabus PDFs or docs — they power TA answers and oral context.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Test before assigning">
    Run a **test submission** yourself with a personal email before assigning to a full class.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Monitor usage">
    Watch usage alerts in the sidebar; purchase more minutes before high-stakes weeks if on a minute-based plan.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Customize Coral's personality">
    Spend 5 minutes customizing your TA's voice, tone, and guardrails to match your teaching style.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
